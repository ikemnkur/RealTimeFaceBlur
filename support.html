📖 About Face Blur

Face Blur is a privacy-focused web application that uses artificial intelligence to automatically detect and blur faces in real-time video streams and uploaded videos. Built with cutting-edge machine learning technology, it runs entirely in your browser for maximum privacy and security.

Our Mission

We believe privacy is a fundamental right. Face Blur empowers individuals, content creators, journalists, and organizations to protect identities while sharing important visual content. Whether you're:

  • A journalist protecting sources and bystanders
  • A content creator respecting audience privacy
  • An educator sharing classroom recordings
  • A security professional redacting CCTV footage
  • An individual protecting your identity online

Face Blur provides a fast, reliable, and secure solution.

🔐 Privacy & Security

Your Data Is Safe

We take privacy seriously. Here's what you should know:

  • No Server Upload: All video processing happens locally in your browser. Your video never leaves your device.
  • No Data Collection: We don't collect, store, or analyze your video data, viewing habits, or personal information.
  • No Tracking: No analytics, cookies, or tracking scripts are used.
  • Secure Connection: Always use HTTPS to access the application.
  • Client-Side AI: The face detection model runs entirely in your browser using TensorFlow.js.

Browser Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

  • Camera Access: Required for webcam mode. You can revoke this at any time in browser settings.
  • File Read: Only used when you explicitly choose to upload a video file.
⚠️ Important: While Face Blur provides strong privacy protection, no automated system is perfect. Always review output before sharing sensitive content. The blur effect should not be relied upon as the sole method of protection for highly sensitive materials.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is Face Blur free to use?

Yes! Face Blur is completely free for personal and non-commercial use.

Do I need to create an account?

No. The app works without any registration or login. Just visit the website and start using it immediately.

What browsers are supported?

Face Blur works on modern versions of Chrome, Firefox, Edge, and Safari. Chrome generally provides the best performance due to optimized WebGL support.

Can I use this on mobile devices?

Yes! The app is responsive and works on tablets and smartphones. However, performance may vary based on device capabilities.

Why is my video not detected?

Ensure your video format is supported (MP4, WebM, AVI, MOV) and that the faces are clearly visible with adequate lighting.

Can I process videos longer than 2 minutes?

The recording feature is limited to 120 seconds to prevent browser memory issues. For longer videos, record in segments and combine them using video editing software.

How accurate is the face detection?

Face Blur uses Google's BlazeFace model, which is highly accurate for frontal faces with good lighting. Side profiles and obscured faces may not be detected consistently.

Can I use this for commercial projects?

For commercial licensing inquiries, please contact us at licensing@faceblur.app

Is my data being analyzed or stored?

No. All processing happens locally in your browser. We have no access to your video data.

Can I contribute to the project?

We welcome contributions! Contact us at contribute@faceblur.app for more information.
